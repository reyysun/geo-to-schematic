GeoToSchematic
Convert KML and GeoJSON files into Minecraft schematics using BuildTheEarth projection!
Elevation handling is supported. All elements that don't have an elevation specified will be positioned at Y level 0.
Import your file:

    ❔ Vertical offset for the entire schematic.
    In case of elevation-specified files, this setting is needed for some BTE servers where the Y level doesn't match the real-world elevation (like -2016 for BTE Italia).
    ❔ If disabled, the vertices of lines and polygons will not be connected and they'll become points.
    Privacy notice: all files are processed by your local machine and aren't uploaded to any server.
